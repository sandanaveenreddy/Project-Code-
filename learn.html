Online Safety Tips

Internet has become one of the integral parts of our daily life. It has transformed the way we communicate, make friends, share updates, play games, and shop. To stay safe in the online world, it is important to follow some cyber-safe practices.

Cyber Awareness Tips

Talk to your children: Discuss the potential online threats such as grooming, bullying, and stalking. Keep track of their online activities and set clear guidelines for internet usage.

Protect from Cyber Grooming: Educate children about responsible use of social media and help them set strong privacy settings.

Update Software: Keep your software and operating system updated with the latest security patches to prevent hacking risks.

Use Parental Controls: Install antivirus software with parental controls to monitor children's online behavior and ensure safe browsing.

Cover Webcams: Protect your privacy by covering webcams when not in use to avoid potential surveillance threats.
