Register as Cyber Volunteer

Preface

For any society to feel safe and secure, citizen support enhances the effect of police efforts. The Cyber Crime Volunteer Program aims to bring together citizens having a passion to serve society in mitigating cyber threats and abuses. Any Indian citizen can associate by registering in any of the three categories of Cyber Volunteer, as mentioned below.

Process of Registration

  1. Any citizen of India may register as a Cyber Volunteer.
  2. To contribute as a Cyber Volunteer, register on the National Cybercrime Reporting Portal.
  3. Create a Login ID: Mention the name of the State/UT of your residence and mobile number (mandatory).
  4. Upload resume, ID Proof, Address Proof, and passport-size photograph.
  5. Select "Type of Volunteer":
    • Cyber Volunteer Unlawful Content Flagger
    • Cyber Awareness Promoter
    • Cyber Expert
  6. Fill "Registration Step 2" and submit.
  7. Save and confirm to move to "Preview and Final Submit."
  8. After "Final Submit," the verification team shall initiate verification.

Acceptable ID and Address Proof

  • Passport, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving License
  • Residence Proof: Passport, Voter ID, Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement, etc.
